Best Plugins For Your WordPress Site

In WordPress, a plugin is a small software application that extends the features and functions of a WordPress site. There are more than 59,000 plugins available for WordPress

Each plugin installed on your site is registered in your WordPress database. You can activate and deactivate any plugin at anytime.

Often beginner do not know which plugin to install for their site. There are many plugins available for SEO, Ecommerce, Form etc. Following are the WordPress best plugins which are providing many features for their users.

Woo Commerce:

WooCommerce is a free WordPress plugin that provides Ecommerce functionality to your WordPress site. By using this plugin your WordPress site becomes fully functional Ecommerce site (with just few clicks). It makes you easily manage your online store, allows to add products, shopping cart, checkout option and much more. It provides all these features at free of cost. WooCommerce is the clear choice for your Ecommerce wordpress site.

Yoast SEO:

Yoast SEO makes sure your site meets the highest technical SEO standards. It also gives you the tools to optimize your content for SEO and overall readability. With the help of Yoast SEO you can improve your content for audience and search engines.




Features Provided by Yoast SEO :

– Create SEO friendly content

– Set up a solid internal linking structure

– Add structured data to help search engine understand your site

– Get integrations with powerful platforms like SEMrush, wincher, elementor, etc.

– 24/7 support

Monster Insight:

monsterInsights provides a simple way to connect your WordPress website to your google analytics account. This makes possible to track traffic, clicks, visitors, statistics and more details of the site on the dashboard. With these data you are able to look what attracts visitors and make them keep coming back to your site and how much time they are spending on your site.

This how Monster Insight looks on the dashboard.

Jet Pack:

JetPack is the best tool for WordPress security, performance and marketing. It is making WordPress site safer and faster and help you grow your traffic. It is all in one solution for social media sharing, statistics and optimization. It is available in a premium or free version.


With Jetpack, you can:


Get a safer, stronger site via secure logins and protection from brute force attacks.

Load pages faster and make your visitors happy with our lightning-fast CDN.

Increase your traffic through automatic social sharing, related content, site search, and customer management.

WP Forms:

WP forms is 100% drag and drop form builder. You can easily create any type of form, from building a simple contact form to complex registration form. It is amongst the most user friendly form plugins available. It provides spam protection and in pro version it is fully customizable. WP forms gives its user way more features which makes it one of the most popular WordPress plugin.

Elementor:

Elementor is a drag and drop page builder for WordPress. It provides all the tools to build your website page and make it more beautiful. It lets you control the design of your whole WordPress site. It is free to use but for premium features the user has to pay.
