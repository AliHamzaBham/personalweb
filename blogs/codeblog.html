5 Best Websites To Learn Coding

Want to learn coding? But don’t know where to learn? Here are the best 5 websites (free and paid) to learn coding. These websites allow you to learn, practice and to be a part of the growing community.

UDEMY:

Udemy is a great code learning platform with over 46 million students. Udemy has a wide variety of categories including web development. Introductory videos are provided for each course so a learner gets an idea of what the course is about. There are huge Udemy learners communities that help you to interact with other learners as well. If you want to make a career out of coding you should visit Udemy.p>

CODE ACADEMY:

Code Academy has almost 45 million users around the globe. The best things about Code Academy is that it provides hands on approach and different paths so you can select your path and master that specific path. User gets an environment to write code of what he/she has learned from courses so far. It’s basically a “Learn by doing” method which makes the coding skills of the learner better and better. Here is the link to the website CodeAcademy

COURSERA:

Coursera was launched 2012 by two Stanford computer professors. There are 82 million learners on the platform with India being the 2nd largest market. They offer range of learning opportunities from hands on approach and courses to job ready certificates and degree programs. Coursera partners with 200 leading universities and industry partners. Visit Coursera.org

UDACITY:

Udacity provides different programs to develop your coding skills and many people have landed their job due to Udacity programs. Udacity has around 200 free courses with 1.6 million users. Udacity students are a community of global learners united in a shared goal of uplift and transformation. You can also be a part of the community. Here is the link udacity.com

UDACITY:

CodeWars is a platform where you can learn, practice and improve your coding skills. You can challenge your self on kata which are small coding exercises. As you complete higher ranked projects you progress through rank. CodeWars is a collective effort by its users. Solving kata that enlighten others and commenting with constructive feedback. It is great community based platform. CodeWars
